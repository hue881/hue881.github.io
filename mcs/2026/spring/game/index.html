How to Play 📖

It works great on one shared screen — a projector, smartboard, or laptop the class can see.

  1. Add players — type each name and press Add. Use 2 or more.
  2. Pick a deck — one lesson, or "All Lessons" for a mixed challenge.
  3. Take turns — the screen shows whose turn it is. Each player gets their own different question; they tap the answer they think is correct.
  4. Score points — a correct answer earns up to 1000 points. The faster you answer, the more you earn!
  5. See the leaderboard after every round, and a winners' podium at the end. 🎉
