🚀 Tier C · Accelerated · Gr. 6–9 · (~25 min build)
🚀 Tier C

Your Task: Build a sprite that reacts to 3 different conditions — including at least one nested conditional. Then write a short AI connection.

🔨 Requirements Checklist
  • Condition 1: if-else — touching edge (say one thing) vs not touching (say another)
  • Condition 2: if-else — key press moves sprite (THEN), or sprite waits (ELSE)
  • Condition 3 (nested): Inside one of the above, add another if-else that checks a second condition. Example: inside "touching edge", check IF space is pressed THEN "I escaped!" ELSE "I'm stuck!"
  • Written reflection: 2 sentences connecting your logic to AI (see below)
