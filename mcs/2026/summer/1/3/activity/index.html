Part 1 of 4 — 10 min, No Screens
Warm-Up: Sequence a Morning Routine
Unplugged Puzzle — Think Before You Touch the Keyboard

These morning routine steps are all out of order. Write the number 1–5 in the circle to put them in the correct order.

Put on your backpack and leave for school
Wake up when the alarm rings
Brush your teeth
Eat breakfast
Get dressed

Now answer these questions:

1. What is the event that starts your morning routine?

2. What happens if you swap steps 2 and 4?

3. In Scratch, the "alarm ringing" would be which block?

A sequence only works correctly if every step is in the right order — just like your morning, and just like a Scratch script!
