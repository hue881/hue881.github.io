📖 Mini-Lesson · 15–20 min
One Big Idea: Algorithm 🤖
🤖 AI Moment — "Are You Smarter Than a Computer?"

A computer can only do exactly what it is told — one step at a time, in order. AI is the same: it follows patterns and rules. The smarter your instructions, the smarter the result.

💡 A precise, ordered list of steps a computer follows is called an ALGORITHM.
📜
Algorithm

A precise list of steps, in order, that solves a task. A recipe is an algorithm for food!

⚡
Instruction

One single step in the list. Computers do each instruction exactly and in order.

🐞
Bug

A mistake in your steps that causes things to go wrong. Bugs are normal — we fix them!
