Before You Begin

What You'll Need — A Quick Checklist

Essentials
✓A parent or guardian's email address
✓A device with a web browser (phone, tablet, or computer)
✓Your child's birth month and year (for age verification)
✓A creative username — no real names!
Privacy Tips
Use a nickname — not your child's real name (e.g., "StarCoder22" not "JohnSmith2015")
The parent email you enter will receive a verification link — check your inbox
No phone number, home address, or school name needed — ever

What is COPPA?
The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (US law, 1998) requires websites to get parental consent before collecting data from kids under 13. Scratch collects your email for this reason — to notify and protect you.

Troubleshooting

Common Problems & Quick Fixes

Username already taken
Add numbers or adjectives: "SuperCoder42" or "BlueRocket99" — the Scratch community has 110M+ users!
Verification email not arriving
Check spam/junk folder. Search for "scratch.mit.edu". Wait 5 min then request again from login screen.
Child forgot password
Click "Sign In" → "Reset Password" → enter parent email. Reset link arrives in under 2 minutes.
Page won't load / errors
Try a different browser. Scratch requires WebGL — update Chrome/Firefox. Check scratch.mit.edu/status for outages.
Need more help? Visit scratch.mit.edu/discuss (Scratch Forums) or email help@scratch.mit.edu — MIT staff respond within 2 business days.
