# 🌟 Our Awesome Classroom Rulebook 🌟

### *How to be a Super Student and an All-Star Friend!* 🦸

Hey there, superstar! 👋 Welcome to our classroom crew. These rules aren't here to be boring — they're our secret recipe for making this the **coolest, kindest, and most fun** place to learn. Ready? Let's go! 🚀

---

## ⏰ Show Up Like a Champ
- **Be on time and ready to roll!** Bring your stuff, bring your brain, and bring your smile. 🎒✨

## 🏃 Indoor Voices, Indoor Speeds
- **No running or screaming** in the classroom or hallway. Save your zoomies and your loudest cheers for the playground! 🤫
- **Stairs and bathrooms are NOT playgrounds.** No playing there — they're for getting where you need to go. 🚶

## 🚽 Bathroom Hero Code
- **Flush it. Clean it. Leave it nice** for the next person. 🌊
- **Wash those hands** super well — bye-bye germs! 🧼👐

## 🚪 Stick With the Squad
- **Never leave the building** without a teacher with you. We keep our whole crew safe and together. 🛡️

## 🍎 Snack Smarts
- **No gum, no glass bottles, and no messy foods.** Keep it clean and keep it safe! 🚫🍬

## 🧹 Keep Our Space Sparkling
- **Pick up your trash** and toss it in the bin. A clean classroom is a happy classroom! 🗑️💫

## 🤒 Rest When You're Sick
- **Feeling feverish or yucky? Stay home and recover.** We'll miss you, but we want you healthy! 🛏️💛

## 🪑 Treat Our Stuff With Care
- **Leave the chairs and desks where they belong.** No moving them around.
- **No drawing on or breaking** desks, chairs, bookcases, windows, or doors.
- **Hands off** the computers, smartboards, books, and papers that aren't yours. If it's not yours, let it be! 🙅

## ✋ Big Brain Mode
- **Listen up during lessons** — you never know what cool thing you'll learn! 🧠
- **Got a question? Raise your hand!** We love curious minds. 🙋

## 📱 Power Down the Gadgets
- **No phones, tablets, or gadgets** during class — unless your teacher says it's tech time. 🔌

## 🍽️ Lunch Bunch Rules
- **Stay with your class** and follow your teacher to your seating spot. We eat together! 🥪

## 🚌 Bus Buddy Manners
- **Listen to staff** when getting on and off the bus.
- **Stay seated** while the bus is moving — no standing or wandering.
- **Be kind to your bus mates!** 😊🚍

## 💛 Kindness Is Our Superpower
- **Be friendly to everyone.** No fighting, no mean words, no foul language. We lift each other UP! 🤝

## 👟 Dress for Adventure
- **Wear safe shoes!** No slippers, flip flops, or wheeled sneakers. Sandals are A-OK. 👡

## 🙏 Respect All Around
- **Respect your teachers and elders.**
- **Celebrate everyone's** traditions and cultures — our differences make us awesome! 🌍🎉

---

### 🏆 The Golden Rule
> **Be kind, stay safe, work hard, and have fun!**
> Follow these and you're officially a Classroom Champion. 🥇

*You've got this! 💪 Let's make this the best year EVER!* 🎊
