# 🌟 Our Awesome Classroom Rulebook 🌟

### *How to Be a Super Student and an All-Star Friend!* 🦸

Hey there, superstar! 👋 Welcome to our classroom crew. These rules are our secret recipe for making this the **coolest, kindest, and most fun** place to learn. Ready? Let's go! 🚀

---

## ⏰ Show Up Like a Champ
Be on time and ready to roll — bring your stuff, your brain, and your smile! 🎒

## 🏃 Indoor Voices, Indoor Speeds
- No running or screaming in the classroom or hallway. 🤫
- Stairs and bathrooms are **not** playgrounds — no playing there!

## 🚽 Bathroom Hero Code
- Flush it, keep it clean, and leave it nice for the next person. 🌊
- Wash your hands super well — bye-bye germs! 🧼

## 🚪 Stick With the Squad
Never leave the building without a teacher. We keep our whole crew safe and together! 🛡️

## 🍎 Snack Smarts
No gum, no glass bottles, and no messy foods. Keep it clean and safe! 🚫

## 🧹 Keep Our Space Sparkling
Pick up your trash and toss it in the bin. A clean classroom is a happy classroom! 🗑️

## 🤒 Rest When You're Sick
Feeling feverish or yucky? Stay home and recover. We'll miss you, but we want you healthy! 💛

## 🪑 Treat Our Stuff With Care
- Leave chairs and desks where they belong — no moving them around.
- No drawing on or breaking desks, chairs, bookcases, windows, or doors.
- Hands off the computers, smartboards, books, and papers that aren't yours. 🙅

## ✋ Big Brain Mode
- Listen up during lessons — you never know what cool thing you'll learn! 🧠
- Got a question? Raise your hand! 🙋

## 📱 Power Down the Gadgets
No phones, tablets, or gadgets during class — unless your teacher says it's tech time. 🔌

## 🍽️ Lunch Bunch Rules
Stay with your class and follow your teacher to your seating spot. We eat together! 🥪

## 🚌 Bus Buddy Manners
- Listen to staff when getting on and off the bus.
- Stay seated while the bus is moving — no standing or wandering.
- Be kind to your bus mates! 😊

## 💛 Kindness Is Our Superpower
Be friendly to everyone. No fighting, no mean words, no foul language. We lift each other UP! 🤝

## 👟 Dress for Adventure
Wear safe shoes — no slippers, flip flops, or wheeled sneakers. Sandals are A-OK! 👡

## 🙏 Respect All Around
Respect your teachers and elders, and celebrate everyone's traditions and cultures. Our differences make us awesome! 🌍

---

## 🏆 The Golden Rule

> **Be kind, stay safe, work hard, and have fun!**
> Follow these and you're officially a Classroom Champion. 🥇

*You've got this! 💪 Let's make this the best year EVER!* 🎊
