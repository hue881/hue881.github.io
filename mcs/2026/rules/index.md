###Rules and Regulations

1. Be on time and prepared for class
2. Do not run or scream in the classroom or the hallway
3. Do not play on the stairs or in the bathrooms
4. Keep the bathrooms clean and flush the toilet after use.  Wash your hands thoroughly
5. Do not leave the building without teacher supervision
6. No gum, glass bottles, or messy food is allowed
7. Keep classroom clean.  Pick up and throw out any trash in the garbage bin
8. Do not come to school when feverish or feeling ill
9. DO not move the chairs and desks.  Do not vandalize desks, chairs, bookcases, windows, or doors.  Do not touch the computers, smartboards, books, papers, or anything in the classroom that does not belong to you
10. Pay attention to lectures. Raise your hand whenever you have a question to ask
11. During class time, no electronic devices are to be used unless specifically instructed
12. During lunch, stay with your class and follow your teacher to the designated seating area
13. Listen to staff when boarding and exiting school buses.  Keep order on buses. Do not stand up or move around while the bus is in motion.  Be kind to your bus mates
14. Be kind and friendly to your peers.  Do not fight.  Do not use foul language
15. Dress properly and safely - no slippers, flip flops, or wheeled sneakers.  Sandals are acceptable
16. Respect your teachers and elders.  Respect others' traditions and cultures
